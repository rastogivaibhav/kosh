# Extraterrestrial Cause for the Cretaceous-Tertiary Extinction

We have observed a massive iridium anomaly at the K-Pg boundary in Gubbio, Italy. 
Iridium is rare on Earth's crust but common in asteroids.
We propose that a massive asteroid impact caused the dinosaur extinction.
