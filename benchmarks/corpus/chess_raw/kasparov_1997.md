# Engine Refutation of Early Prophylaxis
We calculated millions of nodes per second using a deterministic heuristic algorithm. The data contradicts the theoretical model of equilibrium [fischer_1972]. We found that 6.Bg5 exerts too much pressure, causing a chaotic divergence in the ensuing tactical lines. The classical equilibrium is broken. We reject the purely prophylactic approach as it fails under concrete tactical measurement.
