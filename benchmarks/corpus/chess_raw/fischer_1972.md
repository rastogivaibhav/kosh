# Classical Najdorf Sicilian
We calculated the variations for the standard opening: 1.e4 c5 2.Nf3 d6 3.d4 cxd4 4.Nxd4 Nf6 5.Nc3 a6. The measured ratio of wins for black indicates a strong equilibrium. We observed that the classical theory provides a solid foundation. The structural homeostasis is maintained through prophylactic pawn moves.
