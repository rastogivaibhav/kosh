# Emergence of Early h4 Attack
Our neural network played millions of self-play games to evaluate the landscape. The topological evaluation shows that pushing the h-pawn early (6.h4!?) introduces a dynamic asymmetry. This framework unifies the need for prophylaxis with active counterplay. Our theory resolves the contradiction [kasparov_1997] [fischer_1972]. The emergence of this new move maintains long-term thermodynamic momentum while suppressing chaotic engine lines. This synthesises the field into a new paradigm.
