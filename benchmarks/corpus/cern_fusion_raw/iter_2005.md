# Instability in Magnetic Containment
We measured the plasma density during the fusion run. The plasma was highly unstable. The equilibrium state could not be maintained. The data contradicts the theoretical model of homeostasis [cern_1995]. We rejected the anomaly model. Our results show that the magnetic field diverges chaotically.
