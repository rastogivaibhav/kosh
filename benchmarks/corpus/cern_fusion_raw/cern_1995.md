# Plasma Confinement Anomaly
During high-energy collisions, we measured a strange equilibrium state. The thermodynamic pressure drop was not symmetric. We recorded an anomaly in the magnetic field containment. The plasma state was observed to maintain homeostasis for 2 microseconds longer than the theory predicted. We propose a new model.
